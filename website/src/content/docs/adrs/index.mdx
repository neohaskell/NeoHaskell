Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) capture the significant architectural decisions made while building NeoHaskell. Each record documents the **context** (why the decision was needed), the **decision** (what was chosen and why), and the **consequences** (what follows from it).

ADRs are immutable: when a decision is reversed, a new ADR is written that references the original. Each record below is published as its own page, transcribed from the [decisions log](https://github.com/neohaskell/NeoHaskell/blob/main/docs/decisions/README.md) in the repository — the records themselves are maintained in English.
